# SendIt Launches in Ahmedabad

We're thrilled to announce that SendIt is now live in Ahmedabad! After months of preparation and testing, we're ready to serve the vibrant city with our on-demand delivery platform.

## Why Ahmedabad?

Ahmedabad is a bustling city with over 8 million residents, known for its entrepreneurial spirit and growing e-commerce ecosystem. The city's unique blend of traditional businesses and modern startups makes it the perfect place for SendIt to begin its journey.

## What We Offer

- **Express Delivery**: Get your packages delivered within 60 minutes
- **Same-Day Delivery**: Affordable same-day delivery across the city
- **Multiple Vehicle Options**: From bikes for small packages to tempos for larger items
- **Real-Time Tracking**: Track your delivery every step of the way
